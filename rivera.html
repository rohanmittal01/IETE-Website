THE DIAGON ALLEY
DESCRIPTION

Do you have what it takes to prove yourself the "Greatest Potter Head of all time"?There will be 3 rounds:

ROUND-1

In round 1 which is quiz where there will be questions from The Harry Potter Series comprising of 40 questions after which there will be cut off of round 1 to shortlist teams for round 2.

ROUND-2

In round 2 there will be video clips played where the teams need to guess the character or scenario and they have to write down on piece of paper, this will also be scored.

ROUND-3

Teams will be shortlisted for final round in which there will be treasure hunt where there will be 10 clues pointing at 6 different locations and first team to find all items at all locations will be declared winner. 2nd and 3rd will be decided on basis of maximum clues found once winner has found all 6 clues including time as a criteria. In case of tie-breaker there will be rapid fire round and first team to answer will be declared winner.

AnimeniaX
DESCRIPTION

Do you have what it takes to win the greatest battle of Anime all time? There will be 3 rounds:

ROUND 1

In round 1 which there will be video clips played where the teams need to guess the character or scenario or The superpower of the character and they have to write down on piece of paper, this will also be scored after which there will be cut off of round 1 to shortlist teams for round 2.

ROUND 2

Round 2 will be a Buzzer Round between two teams. In this round teams will be asked 25 questions. Half of the teams that have qualified to round two will qualify to round three.

ROUND 3

Teams that will be shortlisted for final round, in which there will be treasure hunt where there will be 10 clues pointing at 6 different locations. So every team has to collect some clues and find the 6 items. When they reach the place where the item is kept. They'll be asked a question. If answered correctly or wrongly their points will be increased or decreased respectively and sent to the other location by giving a clue and first team to find all items at all locations will be declared winner. 2nd and 3rd will be decided on basis of maximum clues found once winner has found all 6 clues. In case of tie-breaker there will be rapid fire round and first team to answer will be declared winner

221B BAKER STREET
DESCRIPTION

221B Baker Street is based on the popular novel, movies and TV series with the titular character of Sherlock Holmes and James Watson. Similar to the plots in the novels, the participants will have to use their ingenuity to solve the challenges presented to them by application of logic and make smart guesses to win the game. There will be two rounds in the event.

ROUND-1

A pen-paper round wherein the participants will be given challenging puzzles to solve. Each question in the set will be a potential clue to a larger puzzle. Points will be awarded for each correct solution. An additional bonus points and a complementary clue to the next round will be given to whichever team or individual is able to co-relate each answer into a larger solution.

ROUND-2

This is a role-play round based on a plot where the participants play the detectives and the organizing team plays the role of the suspects. A room/crime scene will be staged and the participants are expected to interrogate the suspects, analyze clues and have to correctly deduce the true culprit based on facts available to them.
